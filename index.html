About me.

Hey Hello! I'm Jarov Davila! A web page developer, video animation creator, background image designer, professional visual effects creator for videos, among many other things. I'm sure you're thinking how come I really know all this, I don't even know lol, but it's something that I've given a lot of dedication to over time, even if you see this professional page, I only finished this in 3 days haha.

In 2012, this boy came up with the great idea of ​​creating his first YouTube channel "By BL4DERxX", he was a young man with dreams, goals and very ambitious and strict goals, creating, editing and uploading videos on how to install hacks to your games, teaching tricks in games, among others. Over time, evolving and developing more in the world of YouTube, he discovered a software used by professionals called "After Effects", an advanced video editing software, used by many companies that make movies and even video animations.

In 2015, the young man came up with the great idea to study this software, that young man was very curious! Later, he realized how difficult it was, but he did not give up, he investigated and investigated until he could not, until finally creating the famous "Audio Spectrums". At that time, giving away those templates was wonderful, not very common like these days.

After several problems with copyright, By BL4DERxX closed its doors, but someone was born, extremely older, extremely gigantic, more professional than that child who started the internet in ancient times...

Ziromap Designs! Professional creator of visual effects for your videos, there is not much to tell, it is the same but more professional hahaha.

Now this man is learning with other channels much bigger than himself, with up to 2 million followers, and even this is the tip of the iceberg :)
